I've recently fallen in love with capturing a moment in a frame. By accident, through an elective photography course in the second semester of design school, it has changed the way I see the world. I'm always on the lookout for shapes, lines, and texture, for shadows and light. "Cool cloud days" excite me and missed opportunities bum me out. Those around me would agree, I am hooked on photography! All of these images make me feel something, and saying that makes me happy, proud even. I hope you'll stick around for the journey...let's see what happens.
